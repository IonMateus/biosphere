About

BioSphere is a platform developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 that makes it easier to explore NASA bioscience publications. It helps users quickly find relevant information about space experiments, visualize results, and connect papers to datasets and media.

The Challenge

The NASA Space Apps Challenge asks teams to build tools that surface insights from space data. BioSphere focuses on bioscience literature, enabling efficient discovery and browsing of publications and related resources.

  • Search and filter bioscience publications
  • Highlight experiments and key findings
  • Link to datasets, images and full-text where available
